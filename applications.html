APPLICATIONS OF LDR:



  • LIGHTING SWITCH.

    • THE MOST OBVIOUS APPLICATION FOR AN LDR IS TO AUTOMATICALLY TURN ON A LIGHT AT CERTAIN LIGHT LEVEL .
    AN EXAMPLE OF THIS COULD BE A STREET LIGHT




  • CAMERA SHUTTER CONTROL.

    • LDR's CAN BE USED TO CONTROL THE SHUTTER SPEED OF A CAMERA.
    THE LDR WOULD BE USED TO MEASURE THE LIGHT INTENSITY AND SET THE CAMERA SHUTTER SPEED TO THE APPROPRIATE LEVEL.
