DISADVANTAGES OF STREET LIGHT SENSOR:



  • THE AUTOMATIC STREET LIGHT SYSTEM REQUIRES A HIGHER INITIAL INVESTMENT IN COMPARISION TO CONVENTIONAL STREET LIGHTS.


  • RISK OF THEFT OF THE AUTOMATIC STREET LIGHT SYSTEM IS RELATIVELY HIGHER SINCE THEY ARE NON-WIRED AND ARE MUCH EXPENSIVE.


  • RECHARGEABLE BATTERIES OF THE AUTOMATIC STREET LIGHT SYSTEM ARE REQUIRED TO BE REPLACED A FEW TIMES.


  • SNOW, DUST OR MOISTURE CAN ACCUMILATE ON PV PANELS WHICH CAN HINDER ENERGY PRODUCTION.
